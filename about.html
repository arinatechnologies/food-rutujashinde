About Koffeenest

Where passion for coffee meets community spirit

Our Story

2015

First Location Opens

Our founder opened the first Koffeenest in a small downtown space with just 10 seats.

2017

Roastery Added

We began roasting our own beans to ensure the freshest coffee experience.

2019

Second Location

Expanded to our flagship location with full kitchen and event space.

2021

Community Partnership

Launched our initiative to support local farmers and artisans.

2023

Sustainability Commitment

Became the first carbon-neutral coffee shop in our region.
